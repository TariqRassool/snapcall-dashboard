How it works: The SnapCall CSV provides per-agent session counts, CSAT and resolution time. The Total Tickets CSV provides each agent's total assigned ticket volume (via Ticket first assigned). The dashboard joins them on Assignee name + Month to compute Adoption Rate = agent's SnapCalls ÷ agent's own ticket count. Agents assigned to Other teams are automatically excluded from all stats.
